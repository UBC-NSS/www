Lab Focus Areas

Software Engineering for Systems

Modern distributed systems are difficult to reason about. We have developed a set of projects that make the process of instrumenting, debugging, and visualizing distributed systems simpler.

  • Distributed Clocks (vector clock instrumentation library)
  • Dinv, a distributed invariant checker (SOSP '15 Poster)
  • ShiViz/TSViz, visualization engines for distributed executions (ICSE '14, ICSE '17)
  • PGo (A PlusCal spec to Go implemention compiler)

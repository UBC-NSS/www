Networks, Systems, and Security (NSS) Lab

The NSS lab is part of the Department of Computer Science, at the University of British Columbia.

People

NSS Lab is co-led by 6 faculty and includes a number of MSc and PhD students. We are a diverse group and are always looking to recruit strong students to join us.

NSS lab is supported by a number of government and industrial sources, including Cisco Systems, the Communications Security Establishment Canada, Intel Research, the National Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (NSERC), Network Appliance, Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada, and the National Science Foundation (NSF).
